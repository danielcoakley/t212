# Decision Rules

Decision rules are deterministic and conservative. They do not authorize live
execution.

## BUY_NOW

Requires high composite score, conviction, data quality, positive technical
setup, non-extreme valuation, acceptable catalyst risk, upside/downside ratio
of at least 2.0, current price at or below max buy price, and portfolio
improvement context.

## WATCHLIST_WAIT_ENTRY

Used when the thesis is strong but current price, technical extension, or
risk/reward makes entry unattractive.

## WATCHLIST_WAIT_CATALYST

Used when the thesis is promising but needs earnings, filing, product,
regulatory, macro, or margin confirmation.

## REJECT

Used for weak thesis quality, poor data, extreme valuation, broken balance
sheet, unclear catalyst, or unattractive risk/reward.

## HOLD_EXISTING

Reserved for portfolio review where an existing holding remains superior or no
material rebalance trigger exists.
